WHAT IS WORK AND TRAVEL PROGRAM?

What requirements does this program have?
  • Between the ages of 18 and 23
  • Currently enrolled full time in a university (1st, 2nd, 3rd years)
  • Must be tolerant to different religious and other minority values
  • Physically and emotionally fit for working in a fast-paced environment
  • Motivated by the idea of the cultural exchange programs and its values
  • Able to live independently far from their families for 3-4 months

About Us

Since 2008 Global Friends has been the leading Work and Travel program provider in Uzbekistan. We want every student in Uzbekistan to have a chance to experience what it's like to work and travel in the United States of America.
